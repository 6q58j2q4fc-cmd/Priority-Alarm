## Lead Capture System

### Current Implementation
The website captures leads through:
- Contact form on the Contact page
- Compact lead forms in CTAs
- Newsletter signup in footer

### Lead Data Collected
- First Name, Last Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Preferred Neighborhood
- Budget Range
- Timeline
- Project Description

### Recommended CRM Integration
Connect the lead capture forms to a CRM system:

**Option 1: HubSpot (Free tier available)**
- Automatic lead scoring
- Email sequence automation
- Pipeline management

**Option 2: Salesforce**
- Enterprise-level CRM
- Advanced automation
- Custom workflows

**Option 3: Pipedrive**
- Simple, visual pipeline
- Good for small teams
- Affordable pricing

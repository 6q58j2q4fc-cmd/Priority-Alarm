## Best Practices

### Writing Tips

1. **Lead with the news** - Most important info in first paragraph
2. **Use quotes** - Include at least one quote from Kevin Rea
3. **Include keywords** - Naturally incorporate SEO keywords
4. **Keep it concise** - 400-600 words ideal
5. **Add multimedia** - Include images when possible
6. **Include contact info** - Make it easy to reach Kevin

### Formatting Guidelines

- Use standard press release format
- Include dateline (BEND, OREGON – DATE)
- End with ### or -30-
- Include boilerplate about company
- Provide complete contact information

### SEO Optimization

**Include these elements:**
- Primary keyword in headline
- Secondary keywords in subheads
- Location keywords (Bend, Central Oregon)
- Link to website
- Neighborhood names when relevant

**Keyword placement:**
- Headline: Primary keyword
- First paragraph: Primary + location
- Body: Secondary keywords naturally
- Boilerplate: Brand keywords

### Distribution Tips

1. **Timing** - Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM Pacific
2. **Consistency** - Regular schedule builds momentum
3. **Follow up** - Contact local media directly
4. **Track results** - Monitor pickups and traffic
5. **Repurpose** - Turn releases into blog posts

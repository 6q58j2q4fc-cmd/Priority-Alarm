# Automated Lead Generation & Content Strategy Guide

## Rea Co Homes - Central Oregon Custom Home Builder

This comprehensive guide outlines the automated systems and strategies implemented for capturing, nurturing, and converting leads for Kevin Rea's custom home building business in Central Oregon.

---

## Executive Summary

The Rea Co Homes lead generation website is designed to automatically capture leads from potential custom home buyers across Central Oregon. The system includes database-backed lead storage, instant notifications to Kevin Rea, SEO-optimized content, and strategic positioning across all major Central Oregon luxury home communities.

---

## 1. Lead Capture System

### 1.1 How Leads Are Captured

The website features multiple lead capture touchpoints strategically placed throughout the user journey.

| Location | Form Type | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Homepage CTA | Full form | Primary conversion point for engaged visitors |
| Contact Page | Full form | Dedicated inquiry page for serious prospects |
| Neighborhood Pages | Compact form | Capture interest from location-specific searches |
| Blog Sidebar | Newsletter signup | Build email list for nurturing |
| Footer | Quick contact | Catch visitors before they leave |

### 1.2 Lead Data Collected

Each lead submission captures the following information:

- **Required**: First name, last name, email address
- **Optional**: Phone number, preferred neighborhood, budget range, timeline, project description
- **Automatic**: Submission source, timestamp, status tracking

### 1.3 Instant Notification System

When a lead submits the form, Kevin Rea receives an immediate notification containing:

- Full contact details
- Project preferences and budget
- Timeline for building
- Any message or questions from the prospect

**Note**: Notifications are delivered through the Manus notification system. To receive these notifications, ensure you are logged into the Manus platform.

## 2. SEO Strategy for Maximum Lead Capture

### 2.1 Primary Keywords Targeted

The website is optimized for the following high-value search terms:

| Keyword Category | Example Terms | Search Intent |
|-----------------|---------------|---------------|
| Primary Service | "custom home builder bend oregon", "luxury home builder central oregon" | High purchase intent |
| Location-Based | "brasada ranch builder", "tetherow custom homes" | Neighborhood-specific |

| Misspellings | "custome home builder", "bend orgon builder" | Capture overlooked traffic |

### 2.2 Content Strategy for SEO

The blog section contains SEO-optimized articles targeting:

1. **Neighborhood Guides**: Detailed content about each Central Oregon community
2. **Building Process**: Educational content about custom home construction
3. **Market Insights**: Local real estate trends and building costs
4. **Design Inspiration**: Architectural styles popular in the high desert

### 2.3 Technical SEO Implementation

- **robots.txt**: Configured to allow all search engine crawlers
- **sitemap.xml**: Auto-generated sitemap submitted to search engines
- **Meta Tags**: Unique title and description for each page
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data for local business and services

# Google Business Profile Integration Guide

## Rea Co Homes - Local SEO Enhancement

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for setting up and optimizing a Google Business Profile (GBP) for Rea Co Homes to maximize local search visibility in Central Oregon.

---

## Why Google Business Profile Matters

Google Business Profile is essential for local businesses because it directly impacts how your business appears in Google Search and Google Maps. For a custom home builder like Rea Co Homes, a well-optimized GBP can generate significant leads from people searching for builders in Central Oregon.

### Key Benefits

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Local Pack Visibility | Appear in the top 3 local results for "custom home builder Bend Oregon" |
| Google Maps Presence | Show up when people search for builders on Google Maps |
| Rich Snippets | Display ratings, reviews, photos, and contact info directly in search results |
| Direct Actions | Enable click-to-call, directions, and website visits from search results |
| Review Management | Collect and respond to customer reviews to build trust |

---

## Step 1: Claim or Create Your Google Business Profile

### If Rea Co Homes Already Has a Listing

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com/)
2. Sign in with a Google account (preferably kevin@reacohomes.com or a business Gmail)
3. Search for "Rea Co Homes" or "Rea Company Homes"
4. If found, click "Claim this business"
5. Verify ownership via phone call, postcard, or email

### If Creating a New Listing

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com/)
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Enter business name: **Rea Co Homes**
4. Select category: **Custom Home Builder** (primary)
5. Add secondary categories:
   - Home Builder
   - General Contractor
   - Construction Company
6. Enter service area (see Step 2)

## Step 5: Collect and Manage Reviews

Reviews are the most important ranking factor for local SEO.

### Review Collection Strategy

1. **After Project Completion**: Send a personalized email to clients asking for a review
2. **Review Link**: Create a short link to your review page using Google's review link generator
3. **Follow-Up**: If no response in 1 week, send a gentle reminder

### Sample Review Request Email

```
Subject: Thank You for Choosing Rea Co Homes

Dear [Client Name],

It was a pleasure building your custom home at [Location]. We hope you're enjoying your new space!

If you have a moment, we would greatly appreciate a review of your experience with Rea Co Homes. Your feedback helps other families find a trusted builder for their dream home.

[Leave a Review on Google]

Thank you again for trusting us with your home.

Warm regards,
Kevin Rea
Rea Co Homes
541-390-9848
```

### Responding to Reviews

- **Respond to ALL reviews** within 24-48 hours
- Thank positive reviewers by name
- Address negative reviews professionally and offer to resolve issues offline
